# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 01/06/2026

Orbit Swap (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is developed and published by **Just_Another_Dev**.  
We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, shared, and protected when you use the Orbit Swap mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect limited data to support app performance, gameplay features, analytics, and advertising.

### 1.1 App Performance, Diagnostics, and Gameplay Data

Orbit Swap uses **Firebase Analytics**, **Firebase Crashlytics**, and **Firebase services** to collect:

- Crash logs and diagnostics data  
- App performance and stability metrics  
- App usage and gameplay activity (such as levels played, achievements unlocked, leaderboard participation, and daily challenge progress)  
- An **anonymous user identifier** generated automatically on first launch via Firebase Authentication  

This data is used to:
- Identify and fix bugs
- Monitor app health and stability
- Support gameplay features such as leaderboards, achievements, and daily challenges
- Improve overall gameplay experience

Orbit Swap does **not** collect:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Precise location data
- Messages or files
- Device identifiers such as IMEI or MAC address

---

### 1.2 Advertising Data

Orbit Swap displays advertisements using third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob).

These services may automatically collect:

- Advertising identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID)
- Approximate device information (for example, device type and operating system version)
- Ad interaction data (such as impressions and clicks)

This information is used to:
- Display advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Support advertising-related functionality

Advertising data is **not** used by Orbit Swap to personally identify users.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

- **Analytics and diagnostics:** monitoring app usage trends, diagnosing crashes, and improving performance
- **Gameplay functionality:** enabling leaderboards, achievements, and daily challenges
- **Advertising:** displaying ads and measuring ad effectiveness

We do **not** use collected data for:
- Selling user data
- Direct marketing communications
- Personal profiling beyond ad delivery and app analytics

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell user data.

Information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers only as necessary to operate the app, including:

- **Google Firebase** (analytics, crash reporting, authentication, and cloud data storage)
- **Advertising partners** (for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention)

These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

---

## 4. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to:

- Monitor app performance and stability
- Diagnose and resolve technical issues
- Support gameplay features, analytics, and advertising functionality

Retention practices follow the policies of our third-party service providers.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

Orbit Swap does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information.

---

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect collected information, including:

- Using reputable third-party services with industry-standard security practices
- Encrypting data in transit using secure network protocols
- Limiting data collection to what is necessary for app operation

---

## 7. User Choices

Users may control or reset advertising identifiers through their device’s system settings.

Where supported, users can also limit ad personalization through their device or Google account preferences.

---

## 8. Account Creation

Orbit Swap uses **automatic anonymous account creation** to support gameplay features such as progress tracking, leaderboards, achievements, and daily challenges.

- No registration is required
- No username, email address, phone number, or password is requested
- A random anonymous user ID is generated automatically on first launch using Firebase Authentication

---

## 9. Account and Data Deletion

### How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your anonymous Orbit Swap account and associated data, please email:

**ashenoydeveloper@gmail.com**

Use the subject line:

**Account Deletion Request — Orbit Swap**

In your message, please include:
- A statement that you are requesting deletion of your Orbit Swap account and associated data
- The approximate date you last used the app

Because accounts are anonymous, you are **not required to provide a user ID**.

---

### Data that will be deleted

Upon request, we will permanently delete data associated with the anonymous account, including:

- Anonymous account identifier
- Leaderboard entries
- Achievement and progression data
- Daily challenge participation and streak data
- Any in-app profile data associated with the account

---

### Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained in the following cases:

- **Aggregated or de-identified analytics data**, which cannot be linked back to an individual account
- **Crash and diagnostics data**, used to improve app stability, which may not always be removable on a per-account basis
- **Ad-related data** collected by third-party advertising providers, which is governed by their own privacy policies

---

### Processing timeframe

Account deletion requests are typically processed within **30 days**.

# Privacy Policy **Last updated:** 01/06/2026 **Orbit Swap** (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Orbit Swap mobile application. --- ## 1. Information We Collect We collect limited data to support app performance, analytics, and advertising. ### 1.1 App Performance and Diagnostics Data Orbit Swap uses **Firebase Analytics** and **Firebase Crashlytics** to collect: - Crash logs - Diagnostics data - App performance and stability metrics This data is used to identify bugs, monitor app health, and improve overall reliability. We do **not** collect: - Names or email addresses - Precise location data - Messages, files, or user-generated content - Device identifiers such as IMEI or MAC address --- ### 1.2 Advertising Data Orbit Swap displays advertisements using third-party advertising services. These services may automatically collect: - Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID) - Approximate device information (e.g., device type, operating system version) - Ad interaction data (such as impressions and clicks) This information is used to: - Display ads - Measure ad performance - Support advertising-related functionality We do not use advertising data to personally identify users. --- ## 2. How We Use Information Collected information is used for the following purposes: - **Analytics:** monitoring app usage trends, diagnosing crashes, and improving performance - **Advertising or marketing:** displaying ads and measuring ad effectiveness We do **not** use collected data for: - Selling user data - Direct marketing communications - Personal profiling beyond ad delivery --- ## 3. Data Sharing We do **not sell** user data. Information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers **only as necessary to operate the app**, including: - **Google Firebase** (analytics and crash reporting) - **Advertising partners** (for ad delivery and measurement) These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws. --- ## 4. Data Retention Data is retained only for as long as necessary to: - Monitor app performance - Diagnose and resolve technical issues - Support analytics and advertising functionality Retention practices follow the policies of our third-party service providers. --- ## 5. Children’s Privacy Orbit Swap does **not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13**. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information. --- ## 6. Data Security We take reasonable measures to protect collected information, including: - Using reputable third-party services with industry-standard security practices - Limiting data collection to what is necessary for app operation --- ## 7. User Choices Users may control or reset advertising identifiers through their device’s system settings. Where supported, users can also limit ad personalization through their device or Google account preferences. --- ## 8.

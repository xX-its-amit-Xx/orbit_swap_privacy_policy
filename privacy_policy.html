Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

Orbit Swap ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Orbit Swap mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited data to support app performance, analytics, and advertising.

1.1 App Performance and Diagnostics Data

Orbit Swap uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect:

  • Crash logs
  • Diagnostics data
  • App performance and stability metrics

This data is used to identify bugs, monitor app health, and improve overall reliability.

We do not collect:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Precise location data
  • Messages, files, or user-generated content
  • Device identifiers such as IMEI or MAC address

1.2 Advertising Data

Orbit Swap displays advertisements using third-party advertising services. These services may automatically collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate device information (e.g., device type, operating system version)
  • Ad interaction data (such as impressions and clicks)

This information is used to display ads, measure ad performance, and support advertising-related functionality.

We do not use advertising data to personally identify users.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: monitoring app usage trends, diagnosing crashes, and improving performance
  • Advertising or marketing: displaying ads and measuring ad effectiveness

We do not use collected data for selling user data, direct marketing communications, or personal profiling beyond ad delivery.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

Information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers only as necessary to operate the app, including:

  • Google Firebase (analytics and crash reporting)
  • Advertising partners (for ad delivery and measurement)

These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to monitor app performance, diagnose and resolve technical issues, and support analytics and advertising functionality.

Retention practices follow the policies of our third-party service providers.

5. Children's Privacy

Orbit Swap does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect collected information, including using reputable third-party services with industry-standard security practices and limiting data collection to what is necessary for app operation.

7. User Choices

Users may control or reset advertising identifiers through their device's system settings. Where supported, users can also limit ad personalization through their device or Google account preferences.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
